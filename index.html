About

I am an Assistant Professor at BITS Pilani. My research focuses on decision-making under uncertainty, including reinforcement learning, RLHF, multi-agent systems, optimization, game theory, and AI alignment.

News

  • Jan 2025: Paper accepted at AAAI 2025.
  • Aug 2024: Joined BITS Pilani as Assistant Professor.
  • Apr 2023: Presented work at AISTATS 2023, Valencia.

Research Interests

  • Reinforcement Learning and Multi-Agent RL
  • Optimization and Stochastic Approximation
  • Game Theory and Sequential Decision Making
  • RLHF, Robustness, and AI Alignment

Students & Lab

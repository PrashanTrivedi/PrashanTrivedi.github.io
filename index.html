Prashant Trivedi

I am an ML Scientist at One Network Enterprises.

I am also a Ph.D. student at Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (IIT Bombay), working with Prof. N. Hemachandra.

My research interests primarily lie in developing and analyzing efficient and practical algorithms for multi-agent decision making settings by exploiting the underlying problem structures. My recent research has focused on multi-agent reinforcement learning and decision making with special emphasis on learning in non-Euclidean setting, learning with the networked agents, differntial privacy in multi-agent reinforcement learning, and learning the stable partitions of the hedonic games.
